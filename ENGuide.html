Simple "healer Emerald Nightmare guide"

   

Nythendra

1. All healers pick a spot in the room so everyone is in healing-range.

2. If you get Rot move to the edge of the room and prepare for some damage.

3. If a raidmember gets Rot pre-HoT or get ready to heal.

4. When a tanks gets affected by Volatile Rot, get ready to heal when the debuff falls of.

5. If Nythendra casts Infested Breth your direction, move to the side of her.

All times: Don't stand in shit.

   

Ursoc

1. When the tanks hit two stacks of Overwhelm, get your heavy heals ready.

2. When your screen gets all foggy, get away from the boss to the assigned spot. Assigned spot is likely the center of the room.

3. Rigth before Ursoc uses Barrling Impact if you are assigned by raidleader to stand infront of the boss do it, but don't if you already have Momentum debuff.

4. When Ursoc uses Barreling Impact grab your big raid heals and heal.

5. When Ursoc uses Roaring Cacophony grab your big raid heals and heal. He will also leave a DoT-ground after this, move to the side to avoid the damage.

6. Big Healing-CDs will likely be needed for the above abilites when Ursoc is below 30% health, he does 25% more damage. But don't be afarid to use them early if needed.
